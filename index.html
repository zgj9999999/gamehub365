Monster Survivors

Battle against endless waves of monsters in this exciting survival game. Use your skills and strategy to defeat enemies and survive as long as possible in this thrilling adventure!

Game Description

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Collect power-ups, upgrade your character, and set new high scores as you fight to survive in this challenging environment.

How to Play

Use WASD keys or arrow keys to move your character. Your character will automatically attack nearby monsters. Collect experience orbs dropped by defeated monsters to level up and choose new abilities. Survive as long as possible and defeat as many monsters as you can!

Game Features

Endless Gameplay

Face waves of monsters that become increasingly difficult as time passes. How long can you survive?

Character Upgrades

Level up your character and choose from a variety of powerful abilities and upgrades.

Unique Monsters

Battle against different types of monsters, each with their own attack patterns and behaviors.

Power-ups

Collect special items throughout the game to gain temporary advantages against the monster hordes.
